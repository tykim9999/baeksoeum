### Description
```
A bedtime companion for first-time parents and the babies who keep them up at night.

— Grounded in research —
White noise shortens sleep-onset time (PMC11283987). Lullabies aid infant emotional regulation and heart-rate calm (PMC8220405). Consistent bedtime routines reduce night wakings (PMC2675894 RCT, 405 dyads). Tummy time supports gross-motor development and prevents flat-head syndrome (AAP Pediatrics 2020). Every feature is built on evidence like this.

— Features —
• White / pink / brown noise (mathematically synthesized)
• 5 lullabies: Brahms, Mozart, Twinkle Twinkle, Korean traditional, Seomjipagi
• Womb sounds: maternal heartbeat, amniotic flow
• Sleep timer (15/30/60 min, gentle fade-out)
• Sleep log: one-tap start/stop, weekly chart
• Bedtime routine: Korean steps (bath → massage → feed → lullaby → lights out) with streak tracking
• Tummy Time: timer + parent prompts (with high-contrast B&W slideshow on Apple TV)
• Glow Mode: large clock + warm breathing gradient (look at it all night)

— Apple TV too —
The same app runs on Apple TV. Your living-room TV becomes the nursery's warm light: a big clock, tummy-time visual stimulation, lullaby playback.

— Privacy —
All data stays on your device. No ads, no analytics, nothing sent to a server. iCloud sync stays inside your private container.

— Designed to respect tired parents —
Dark, warm colors that don't blind you at 2am. One-handed operation. Big touch targets and haptics for the dark.

This is not a medical device. Consult your pediatrician for any concerns about your child's health or development.
```
